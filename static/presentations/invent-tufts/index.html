What have we been up to?

Let's quickly introduce who's who, what's what and why stuff is the way it is.

PyScript is a platform for Python in the browser.

We bring together two of the world's most vibrant tech cultures.

If stuff runs on Python, and now Python runs on the web, now stuff works everywhere there's a browser (which is everywhere).

Who is PyScript for..?

In terms of the platform... it's for programmers.

It's powerful, efficient and empowering for anyone who is vaguely technical ALREADY.
