PyScript is a platform for Python in the browser.

We bring together two of the world's most vibrant tech cultures.

If stuff runs on Python, and now Python runs on the web, now stuff works everywhere there's a browser (which is everywhere).

PyScript.com is a website providing PyScript related services.

You have code in the neighbourhood... who ya gonna call?

  • It has all the PyScript gizmos you may need.
  • It gets you and your PyScript apps to the places you need to go (it's a hosting platform).
  • It also has a whole bunch of complementary technology to make it easier to do your job with PyScript (API proxy).
  • It provides services, such as storage, versioning and code sharing, so your life is easy and convenient.

Invent is a PyScript based app creation framework with complementary browser based tooling and is designed to be easy to learn and use, no matter if you're a beginner or expert.

It's all about invention, imagination and empowering folks to build cool stuff that addresses their own unique needs and requirements...

  • The focus is on playful creativity that facilitates valuable improvisation.
  • We want folks to imagine and think up ideas, focusing on possibilities and solving the stuff that matters to them (not some esoteric technical faff).
  • Invent gives you the ingredients, tools and processes needed to quickly cook up the things you need to serve.
  • It is extensible, it can grow with you.
  • Finally, since it complements PyScript.com as a platform to publish and share your work with the wider world.
